Farewell to the HSAB Treatment of Ambident Reactivity
DOI: 10.1002/anie.201007100

This is an important artical that contains a overall review of how Marcus theory is used in organic group transfer reactions.
Works as a index to Very Important Paper(VIP) s.

Understanding

In “2.4 Marcus Theory”:

  • Parabolic：

    “In the case of group‐transfer reactions, a major contribution to the parabolic term comes from the A-X and B-X vibrations.””
    1.[VIP]10.1146/annurev.pc.31.100180.001303
    这是个独立的假设？貌似与Marcus原本的概念(整个体系的势能面截面)不自洽 #引文中没找到相关陈述

  • $\Delta G_{int}^{\neq}$ Form:

“Marcus suggested calculating the intrinsic barrier of a non‐identity reaction as the average of the two corresponding identity reactions.”
1.[VIP]R. A. Marcus, J. Am. Chem. Soc. 1969, 91, 7224–7225
2.S. S. Shaik, H. B. Schlegel, P. Wolfe, Theoretical Aspects of Physical Organic Chemistry: The SN2 Mechanism, Wiley, New York, 1992.(written paper! OMG!)
这是公认的处理？在2020年10.1021/acs.jpca.0c03933中没有使用 #确实是公认的处理，引文并不是最初提出的文献，追溯到R. A. Marcus, J. Phys. Chem., 72, 891 (1968)线索断了，此文开头直接使用了该结论。没有任何相关的解释。提到在接近0的时候不适用。在原Marcus模型提出的一篇综述中找到了理论依据，在marcus系列论文V中对重组能进行了讨论，经过对力常数矩阵的一定忽略，可以得到additivity law。

“The validity of this approach has been confirmed computationally and experimentally by several research groups.”
[VIPinVIP] J. M. Gonzales, W. D. Allen, H. F. Schaefer III, J. Phys. Chem. A 2005, 109, 10613–10628.
验证了在简单Sn2反应中的可行性。但是确实有特例。为什么用的是Eb？看看对应用前景的描述？以及引文。

Factors affect the $\Delta G_{int}^{\neq}$

In “2.5”:

  • Hoz Rule

    • Inter-Group Rationalization:

      “Uggerud correlated this trend with the ionization energy of the nucleophile X, and rationalized that those nucleophiles that form bonds to carbon atoms with stronger electrostatic character give rise to lower barriers because of decreased electron repulsion in the transition state.”
      E. Uggerud, J. Phys. Org. Chem. 2006, 19, 461–466.
      这个解释不太可靠，不过提供了一个思路，可以通过考虑三中心键的稳定性来分析TS稳定性

    • Intra-Group:

      “When moving from the top to the bottom within the periodic table, the C-X bond length increases, thereby leading to increasing separation of the parabolas and a rise in the energy of the transition state. At the same time, the force constants decrease and cause a flattening of the parabola and a lowering of the transition‐state energy. Both effects clearly compensate each other and result in almost constant values”
      L. G. Arnaut, S. J. Formosinho, Chem. Eur. J. 2007, 13, 8018–8028.
      基于ISM理论，并不是直接从基本的结构-能量关系出发的，过度简化难以描述中心原子和取代基变化带来的影响

  • Principle of Least Nuclear Motion (PLNM)
    J. Hine, Adv. Phys. Org. Chem. 1977, 15, 1–61.
    纯定性的/概念性的一个基于重组能图像的原理，即始末态结构差距越小，转移离子的位移越小，本征能垒越小
    这个概念的使用是基于曲线的意义是势能面截面的，与键振动的假设不一致
    应用也很少
