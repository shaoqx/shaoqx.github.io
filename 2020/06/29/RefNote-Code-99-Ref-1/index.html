alpha Effect

邻位孤对电子效应

Reactivity enhancement of the nucleophiles brought by the lone pair in the alpha position(e.g. HOO-,H2NN2H). Comparison can be made between reagents that has similar basicity but with and without lone pair.(e.g. n-type nucleophile vs pi-type nucleophile)
